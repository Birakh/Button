Core principles
🎯
Non-neediness
The foundation of everything
Attraction drops the moment approval-seeking becomes visible. Every message you send should imply you have a life and options.
⚖️
Mirror her energy
Never exceed her investment level
If she sends 3 words, do not send 3 paragraphs. Jumping ahead emotionally creates pressure.
👁️
Specificity
The single biggest trust builder
Referencing something she actually said proves you were listening. Generic replies feel like copy-paste.
✂️
Brevity signals confidence
Short is stronger
Long messages signal insecurity. Short, well-chosen words signal you are comfortable with space.
Most common mistakes
Double texting from anxiety
Sending a follow-up because she has not replied signals you are monitoring her. One message. Wait.
Over-explaining when she goes cold
When the energy drops, the instinct is to explain or apologise. This signals guilt and neediness.
Failing a test by engaging with it
When she says something provocative, most men get defensive. Bond acknowledges it with calm amusement.
Reading her messages
Very interested
Long replies, asks questions, references past things you said.
Interested
Consistent replies, some questions, warm tone.
Neutral
Short replies, answers without asking her own.
Testing you
Provocative comment, goes cold after warm.
Going cold
Shorter replies, slower, one word answers.
Before you hit send
Red flags in your own message
  • xStarts with "Hey" and nothing else
  • xUses "just" — "just checking", "just wanted to say"
  • xEnds with "haha" when nothing is funny
  • xMore than 3 sentences
  • xTwo questions in one message
  • xAsks about something she already told you
  • xContains "if you want" or "no worries if not"
